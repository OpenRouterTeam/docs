### Product changes

- **Bring-your-own-key management API** -- New REST endpoints for programmatically listing, retrieving, creating, updating, and deleting [BYOK credentials](/api/api-reference/byok/create-a-byok-provider-credential) across workspaces via management key authentication.
- **Observability destinations management API** -- New REST endpoints for listing, retrieving, creating, updating, and deleting [observability destinations](/api/api-reference/observability/create-an-observability-destination) (Datadog, Langfuse, LangSmith, and more) via management key authentication.
- **Fixed: dashboard growth percentages exceeding display bounds** -- Very large growth percentages on the [activity dashboard](https://openrouter.ai/activity) are now capped at >999% for readability.
