### Product changes

- **Video generation in Playground** -- The [Playground](https://openrouter.ai/playground) now supports video generation with an async generation API, letting you generate and preview videos directly in the UI.
- **Response caching for Responses API** -- Request caching is now supported on the `/responses` endpoint. See [Response Caching docs](/guides/features/response-caching).
- **API tab rename** -- The "Quickstart" tab on model pages has been renamed to "API" for clarity.
